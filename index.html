Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 21, 2024

StreamLines is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or information from users.

2. Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

Our app requires auto-renewable subscriptions for use. These subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store / Google Play Store, and we do not have access to your payment information. For more information on how your payment information is handled, please refer to Apple's / Google's privacy policy.

3. GET Requests to Download Pictures

Our app sends GET requests to an HTTP website for the purpose of downloading pictures. These requests do not contain any personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that would collect or process your data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at streamlines-privacy-policy@outlook.com.
